Tiara Burtin's Resume


An Overview Of The Work Done In The Life Of Tiara



Positions

Tiara Burtin graduated from North County Tech High School in where she became certified in Homeland Security in 2014. She began working at the St. Louis City Justice Center in 2014 as a Data Analysis. She learned how to input confidential information of inmates for the city so that individuals can be processed for court, release, or transfer.


In 2015, Tiara began working as an Educational Policy Intern with The Scholarship Foundation of St. Louis. Tiara assisted the organization in prioritizing the voices of first generatation, low-income college students as the experts in the policies that effect their acessibility to higher education. Tiara organized student bodies to take several trips to Jefferson City for the release of a white paper about the Bright Flight Scholarship.
Tiara was promoted to Lead Policy Intern in 2017 where she also served on the committee to organize the state-wide conference. Tiara learned skills about team building, leadership, budget management, project management, and workshop leadership. She was not only able to develop these skills through training, but she was able to immediately apply them in her leadership role.




Tiara's supervisor began seeing Tiara's passion for wellness in non-profit work and advised her to explore wellness as a form of radical activism. Tiara receieved another internship to explore new forms of advocacy work as a wellness worker through St. Louis Metro Market and Good Life Growing in 2016.
During this time, Tiara learned the back-end and front-end of gardening and retail. At Good Life Growing, Tiara learned gardening skills and how to sell crops to stores. Tiara worked with local stores and learned how to write invoices as a vendor.


At St. Louis Metro Market, Tiara began exploring marketing and graphic design. She came the lead intern of the newsletters, social media graphics, and informational flyers for the community. Tiara connected with government officals to organize pop-up schedules for the truck to visit neighbors to supply healthy food options for the community.

In 2023, Tiara wanted to get back into the community while doing something she enjoyed. she began working at Teatopia, a teashop on Cherokee in St. Louis, Missouri. Tiara deepend her knowledge of herbalism with tea properties and brewing skills. Tiara used her artistic skills to create signs and art work to decorate the store. Tiara mastered customer service skills, team building, and efficent work ethic.


Trades & Education

In 2016, Tiara began pursing her passion for wellness through education through certifcations and entrepenuership. Tiara has had years of experience of self teaching, problem solving, and leadership development on her journey.
Tiara became certified as a Naturopathic Herbalist through A Life of Peace. She began healing herself of stress-related disorders she was battling with. Through mindfulness, diet, and hormonal balancing, Tiara began reprogramming her nervous system to experience more parasympathetic and life sustaining ways of being.
Tiara became certified as a Birth Doula in 2016. She began helping Black families become educated on the process of birth, their rights as birthing people, their options in birth, and creating a birthing plan to ensure their child comes into this world in the ways they see best. Tiara expanded herself to be a Birth Worker as she began offering her clients herbalism services, yoga serives, and reiki healing.



In 2020, Tiara became certified as a yoga instructor with paths in Vinyasa, Yin, Trauma Infromed, and Kundalini. Tiara began hosting her own retreats in Belize to help folks heal their mind, body, and spirit through enviornmental factors, herbalism, and somatic practices. Her retreats consist of gardening, fasting,movement, meditation, and sound therapy.



In 2020, Tiara became certified as a teaching artist. She developed skills to be able to take the states cirriculum and education goals based on subject and age and create art lessonns to teach those subjects. This opened Tiara's eyes to different ways to teach subjects. In 2021, Tiara got her first art residency with Project Art in Pittsburgh teaching 5th graders art twice a week for the Fall and Spring semester. She also began teaching art for for Kamali, a African Centered Homeschool.



In 2021, Tiara was featured in her first exhibit at Jacoby Arts for the Rise Exhibit for Artists of Color. This exhibition opened the door for her to recieve a residency with Sibyls Shrine, a collective of artist who idenify as mothers. Tiara's ability to teach subjects in an artistic way and continue to advance her people skills has allowed alot of opportunities to come her way through discipline



In 2022, Tiara became certified as Master Gardener through Mizzou and The Missouri Botanical Gardener. Through the course Tiara volunteered for 40 hours at gardens internationally, nationally, and locally. She learned about landscaping, different plant properties, soil chemistry, and plant anatomy.



She's passionate about the community having access to healthy foods, mental health resources, alternative forms of healing, and naturopathic medicine for their homes. The mission of the garden is to create a self sustainable, self determined, and self sufficent community. When people leanr that you can make life come from nothing but a seed, water, attention, and love, it changes your outlook on what is possible for life... all thing! All things are possible!




Tiara is currenty becoming trained to break into the techn industry as a Web Developer. Tiara is passionate about using her artistic skills to help business create the foundation they need to operate successful businesses. Tiara is passionate about creating web pages and tech programs to be trauma informed for folks with disabilities and/or neurodivergencies. Tiara wants to bring culture into tech spaces and show other Black women that entering into tech is possible. They are smart enough and they are enough as they show up.
Tiara is interested in climbing the ladder to secure a remote position in the tech industry so that she can travel the world for yogic missionary work. Tiara is inspired through teaching. As a person who has taught herself many skills, she enjoys teaching others. Tiara is also open to securing a position as a teacher for Black womena and men to enter into tech spaces.
Tiara is excited for her future in tech by way of LaunchCode!
